Safe practices for social media users

10 simple tips to stay safe online

  • Think before you share: Try not to post things like your address, phone number, or other private stuff.
  • Use a strong password: A longer password with symbols and numbers is harder to guess.
  • Be careful with links: If a link looks weird or unexpected, it’s best not to click it.
  • Check your privacy settings: Make sure only the right people can see your posts and info.
  • Keep your apps updated: Updates help fix bugs and protect your account.
  • Limit who can access your account: Only share login info if you really need to (and only with people you trust).
  • Watch your account activity: Keep an eye on logins or messages that don’t seem like they came from you.
  • Look out for scams: Don’t fall for messages offering free money or fake giveaways.
  • Don’t overshare: It’s okay to keep some things private — not everything needs to be posted.
  • Use a VPN if you can: It helps keep your internet connection more private and secure.
